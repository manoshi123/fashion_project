Color Confidence: How to Mix and Match Like a Stylist

Playing with color can feel intimidating, but when done right, it can transform your look and boost your confidence. Start by learning basic color theory—complementary colors (like blue and orange) make bold statements, while analogous shades (like pink and red) create a soft, cohesive look. Neutrals like beige, white, black, and gray act as anchors, allowing brighter colors to shine.
